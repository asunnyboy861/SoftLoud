# App Store Metadata

## Promotional Text
Each alarm gets its own volume. Wake up loud, stay reminded soft - independent volume control for every alarm.

## Description
Tired of choosing between a loud morning alarm and a quiet phone at night? On iPhone, all alarms share the same "Ringtone and Alerts" volume. SoftLoud finally fixes this.

Each alarm has its own independent volume setting. Set a loud 7:00 AM wake-up, a soft noon medication reminder, and a medium 5:00 PM meeting alert - all in one place, without changing your system volume or waking the whole house.

WHY SOFTLOUD?

The built-in Clock app ties every alarm to a single system volume. Turn it down at night and you oversleep. Turn it up for your morning alarm and you blast your partner awake. SoftLoud gives each alarm its own volume, so every alert plays at exactly the right level - no compromises.

KEY FEATURES

Independent Volume Per Alarm
Each alarm has its own volume slider from 0% to 100%, completely separate from your iPhone's ringer volume. Your system volume stays exactly where you want it.

Volume Presets
Four built-in presets for quick setup:
- Gentle Wake: 30% with gradual ramp-up
- Normal: 70% steady volume
- Loud and Clear: 100% with gradual ramp-up
- Earthquake: 100% instant full blast

Gradual Volume
Wake up gently. SoftLoud slowly increases the alarm sound from silent to your set volume over 10 to 120 seconds. Choose a gentle sunrise-like experience or a quick ramp-up.

Works on Silent
SoftLoud uses the audio playback category, so your alarms ring even when your iPhone is on silent or in Do Not Disturb mode. Never miss an important alarm again.

Background Audio
Alarms play reliably even when the app is in the background. Just set it and forget it.

Custom Labels
Name each alarm so you know exactly what it is - "Morning Workout", "Take Vitamins", "Pick Up Kids".

Repeat Options
Set alarms for specific days of the week or use them once. Your schedule, your rules.

Clean, Simple Design
No ads, no subscriptions, no tracking. A focused alarm clock that does one thing exceptionally well.

WHO IS SOFTLOUD FOR?

- Heavy sleepers who need a loud alarm but share a room
- Parents who want a soft reminder without waking the baby
- Shift workers with different volume needs at different times
- Anyone who has ever overslept because they turned down their phone volume
- Students in dorms who need to wake up without disturbing roommates

SoftLoud is a one-time purchase. No ads, no subscriptions, no data collection. Your alarm data stays on your device.
