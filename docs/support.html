Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set an alarm with independent volume?

Tap the + button to create a new alarm, then adjust the volume slider to your desired level. Each alarm has its own volume setting that works independently from your iPhone's ringer volume.

What are volume presets?

SoftLoud includes 4 built-in presets: Gentle Wake (30%, gradual), Normal (70%), Loud & Clear (100%, gradual), and Earthquake (100%, instant). Tap any preset to quickly apply it to your alarm.

What is gradual volume?

Gradual volume slowly increases the alarm sound from silent to your set volume over a period you choose (10-120 seconds). This provides a gentler waking experience.

Will my alarm ring if my phone is on silent?

Yes! SoftLoud uses the audio playback category, so your alarms will ring even when your iPhone's ringer is set to silent or Do Not Disturb mode.

How do I delete an alarm?

Swipe left on any alarm in the list and tap the Delete button.

Does SoftLoud work in the background?

Yes, SoftLoud is configured to play audio in the background, so your alarms will ring even when the app is not in the foreground.
