What is SoftLoud?

SoftLoud is an iOS alarm clock app that gives each alarm its own independent volume control. No more oversleeping because you turned down your ringer, and no more being jolted awake at full volume when you wanted a gentle wake-up.

Key Features

Independent Volume
Each alarm has its own volume setting, completely separate from your iPhone's ringer volume.
Volume Presets
Four built-in presets: Gentle Wake, Normal, Loud & Clear, and Earthquake.
Gradual Volume
Slowly ramp up from silent to your set volume for a gentler waking experience.
Background Audio
Alarms ring even when your phone is on silent or Do Not Disturb.
