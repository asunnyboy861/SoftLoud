Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Introduction

SoftLoud ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

SoftLoud does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your alarm data, including alarm times, volume settings, sound preferences, and labels, is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We have no access to this data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Audio recordings
  • Contacts or calendar data

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data usage to describe. All app functionality operates entirely on your device.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is sent to any external servers. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

SoftLoud does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

SoftLoud does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, our app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Retention

All data is stored only on your device for as long as the app is installed. Deleting the app removes all data permanently.

Your Rights

Since SoftLoud stores no data externally, you have complete control over your information. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
