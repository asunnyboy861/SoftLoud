# Pricing Configuration

## Monetization Model: Paid Download

- **Price**: $2.99 (One-time purchase)
- **IAP**: Not required
- **Subscription**: None

## App Store Connect Pricing
- **Price Tier**: Tier 3 ($2.99)

## Rationale
- Simple, focused app with no ongoing costs (no API, no server, no AI)
- Users have high willingness to pay for solving the alarm volume pain point
- "Impulse purchase" price point: less than a cup of coffee
- No subscription fatigue: differentiated from Alarmy ($4.99/mo) and Sleep Cycle ($9.99/mo)
- Zero operational cost means nearly 100% profit per download

## Included Features (All included, no hidden costs)
- Unlimited alarms
- Independent volume control per alarm
- 4 built-in volume presets (Gentle Wake, Normal, Loud & Clear, Earthquake)
- Gradual volume ramp-up
- 8 high-quality alarm sounds
- Custom alarm labels
- Snooze functionality
- Repeat alarms
- All future updates

## Policy Pages Required
- Support Page: ✅ Required
- Privacy Policy: ✅ Required
- Terms of Use: ❌ Not needed (not a subscription app)
